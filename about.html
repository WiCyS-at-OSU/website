WiCyS Student Chapter at Oregon State University

Who are we?

We are the official Women in Cybersecurity (or WiCyS for short) chapter at Oregon State University.

What is our purpose?

Our mission is to build a community within Oregon State University that promotes women's education, participation, and leadership in the field of cybersecurity. In addition, we have the following purposes:

  1. To decrease the gender disparity in cybersecurity.
  2. To provide education, mentorship, and networking support to students through the WiCyS community.
  3. To promote and recruit women in the cybersecurity workforce through Oregon State University.

Who can join?

Membership to WiCyS at OSU is open to all students within Oregon State University without any restrictions. This club is not exclusive to women or those who idenfiy as women, we accept all allies!

Why should I join?

  • Access to Resources and Opportunities: Get early access to scholarships, internships, and jobs through our exclusive mailing list.
  • Skill Development: Enhance both your leadership and technical skills beyond what the classroom offers.
  • Networking and Friendships: Engage in a community that not only opens doors to mentorship and career opportunities but also helps you forge lasting friendships with like-minded individuals.

What will the meetings be like?

Our monthly meetings (start date TBD) will heavily rely on member feedback. We hope to offer a wide range of activities from educational series and special topics to industry expert talks and creative gatherings. For instance, here are some of our ideas:

  • BurpSuite and Metasploit demos
  • Hashcat & John the Ripper demos
  • SQL and XSS injections demos
  • Networking fundamentals
  • Python programming
  • Intro to Capture the Flag (CTF)
  • Industry and professional speakers
  • Game night
  • Cybersecurity jeopardy
  • Move night
  • Ice cream/boba socials
